Synosis

To have freedom, to witness the world beyound Maria Wall, young Eren Yeager and his bestfreinds: Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlelt, promise to acquire the desirable future together.

Before even started, Wall Maria, their hometown, families, and neighbors have tragically been destroyed by the massive humanoid called Titan.
